In a 'normal' solution the models would be in their own project (Dcf.Wwp.Models),
but because Phase I used an EF-DatabaseFirst approach with EDMX files and a bunch of
other stuff - and Phase II is switching to a cleaner and easier Code-First approach,
we're placing the CF models in this project. It just helps keep the approaches and
phases separate, air-tight, and less confusing than having two projects named
Dcf.Wwp.Model and Dcf.Wwp.Models, or Dcf.Wwp.Model.PhaseII or other variations.
